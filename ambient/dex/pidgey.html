Pidgey

Pidgey is a Flying-type Pokémon.

It evolves into Pidgeotto starting at level 18, which evolves into Pidgeot starting at level 36.

In other regions, Pidgey is a Normal-type and Flying-type Pokémon.

Pidgey fly in flocks of ten or more. Rival with Spearow and Fearow. Used to carry mail. Foundin towncenters or fields. Among all Flying-type Pokémon, it is the gentlest and easiest to capture. A perfect choice for a starter Pokémon. A common sight in forest. Very docile. It uses its wings to toss blinding sand to protect itself. If at all possible, it will try to avoid battles. Small or weak enemies are blown away by its whirlwind. It hides in tall grass. Common in grasslands.

Moves: Gust, Sand-Attack, Talon, Gust Back, Flock Strike
