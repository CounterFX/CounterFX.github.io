Tirtouga

During ancient times of Serene, Tirtouga is a Water-type Pokémon.

It evolves into Carracosta starting at level 37.

In other regions, Tirtouga is a Water-type and Rock-type Fossil Pokémon.

It can be resurrected from a Cover Fossil.

Tirtouga is an ancient Pokémon and is said to have lived 100 million years ago. Tirtouga's shell is extremely tough. It is a skillful swimmer. It is found onshore along beaches and in warm-temperatures seas. It can dive underwater beyond half-mile depths. It is occasionally found inland to search for prey. It is said to be the ancestor of most turtle-like Pokémon.

Tirtouga is found buried in the sands along beaches, swimming in the sea, and along the ocean floor. Tirtouga existed during the past.

Ability: Prehistoric Memory

Moves: Archaic Water, Flipper Hit, Relic.
