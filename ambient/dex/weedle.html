Weedle

Weedle is a Bug-type and Poison-type Pokémon.

It evolves into Kakuna starting at level 10, which evolves into Beedrill starting at level 15.

In other regions, Weedle evolves into Kakuna starting at level 7, which evolve into Beedrill starting at level 10.

Weedle is a small and fast highly-capable fighter. Its colorful body is intended to warn off its enemies. It has a sharp two-inch venomous stinger on its head that injects a strong poison. This stinger has a high accuracy and deals powerful damage. It is often found in forests or along grasslands, in trees or bushes under the leaves or fruits its eating. It eats its weight in food every day. It has an extremely acute sense of smell, capable of distinguishing which foods it favors over others. It is known to consume cardboard.

Weedle is a native Pokémon to the Serene region. It is smaller in size than those of other regions. It consumes mostly fruits and berries. It has to eat more food because of its size and takes longer to progress through evolution. It is commonly referred to as the "fruit worm." It is extremely territorial and dangerous.

Moves: Antenna Horn, Toxin Barb, String Shot
