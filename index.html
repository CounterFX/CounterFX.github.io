Project Ambient - News

Welcome to the official Project Ambient page! This website showcases the ideas, artwork, music, and data about the project. The concept is to create a new world full of beautiful creatures, marvelous and daring characters, tropical and vivid landscapes, and a dramatic memorable adventure. This website will be constantly updated with new information.

About the Director

Counter - aka SereneAmbience aka CounterFX - is the founder, director, artist, and lead developer of this project. He started Project Ambient while sitting in his college lecture hall in 2012. Inspired by his boredom, he began filling in his notebooks with ideas and concept of his favorite series, Pokémon. Several spiral-bound notebooks filled with information, concepts, plots, and artwork were ready to start his dream of creating an actual game. The game was originally designed to be constructed on the Essentials kit for RPG Maker XP but later discontinued due to scheduling conflicts with time and life. Project Ambient is still in concept and anyone is welcome to join/help distribute to this dream!

Want to contribute to this project? Are you an artist? Do you make music? Just want to express your suggestions? Visit the Contact page to share your ideas! Any contributed items or ideas will be credited even if not used.
